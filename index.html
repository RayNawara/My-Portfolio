About Me

I'm Ray Nawara, a full-time AI & Data Warehouse Architect specializing in building AI-assisted intelligent analytics systems.

At a national automotive digital marketing company, I identified that their greatest untapped asset was a massive repository of automobile dealer data. I proposed the solution to the CEO and became the sole architect and implementor of a comprehensive PostgreSQL 18 data warehouse that integrates data from multiple industry platforms including VinSolutions, Drive Centric, Agile CRM, vAuto, CallRail, GA4, Google Ads, and Facebook Ads.

What sets this system apart is the AI layer. I designed and built a multi-persona AI analysis system that doesn't just answer questions like "How many leads did we get this week?" but provides complete performance analysis through specialized AI personas: Sales Performance Analyst, Digital Marketing Analyst, BDC Operations Analyst, Inventory & Merchandising Analyst, and a Synthesis Summarization Analyst that ties it all together. This system is going to Beta and will be offered to automobile dealerships nationwide.

Current Tech Stack: Rails 8.1, Ruby 4.0, PostgreSQL 18, Hotwire (Turbo/Stimulus), Kamal deployment, Solid Queue/Cache/Cable, Node.js for ETL, Google Cloud (BigQuery), Microsoft Azure. I work with AI/LLM integration daily, building customizable prompt systems with hierarchical inheritance for dealer-specific customization.

Background: After 25+ years as a software company CEO managing 30+ offices, I returned to my first love: building software. I combine decades of executive-level business experience with hands-on technical expertise - I understand both the code and the business value it creates. Former Database Administrator, AWS Certified Cloud Practitioner. I work remotely and maintain a powerful local AI development environment for experimentation.
